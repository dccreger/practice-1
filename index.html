Cake Sizes

Round Cakes come in 6" and 8" sizes with 3 layers of cake. 6" Cakes feed 10-12 people - perfect for small gatherings. 8" Cakes feed up to 25 people.

Tiered Cakes come with an 8" bottom tier and a 6" top tier, each with 3 layers of cake. Feel free to add a different layer for each tier. Perfect for weddings or large gatherings.

Specialty Cup Cakes are also available for order. Minumum of 2 dozen.
